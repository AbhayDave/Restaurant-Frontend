OUR STORY

SERVED EVERY DAY SINCE 1990

BHOOKEMONS was opened on Friendship Day 2016. Chef / Owner John Wick began making sweets and spicy dishes and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Tribeca. Today, NYC’s beloved restaurant celebrates 7 years of classic, made from scratch Indian cooking.
